    Lex Latin- Borr Drilling is awarded a services contract by Pemex

    With experts in a very broad range of legal areas, Von Wobeser y Sierra has the capacity and structure necessary to rapidly put together multidisciplinary teams tailor-made for each matter concerning Corporate, Mergers & Acquisitions.

    We are, in the most precise sense of the word, a full service firm.

    The corporate, mergers & acquisitions lawyers work hand in hand with the experts of the firm in tax, labor, real estate, anti-corruption, competition and antitrust, intellectual property, regulatory, disputes and litigation and other specialty areas to provide full, high quality services. With eight partners and more than twenty five lawyers, the practice brings together a repertory of experience and reaches a degree of sophistication that few firms in Mexico can match. The services of Von Wobeser y Sierra focus on responsiveness, creativity, attention to detail, a pragmatic and business oriented approach and professionalism. Client satisfaction is fundamental for us. We understand that each corporate matter, regardless of its size, is important. Our client list includes some of the largest companies in the world, leaders in their industries, and we are often part of the most complex and high profile transactions occurring in Mexico and Latin America. However, we also represent start-ups, family and private companies, and growing clients that do not normally carry out mergers and acquisitions or other complex corporate transactions. Our experience in the area covers corporate transactions and matters of different magnitudes, both cross border and multijurisdictional as well as national, in a broad range of industries: (1) mergers, acquisitions and other corporate transactions of public companies; (2) leveraged buy-outs and management buy-outs; (3) private capital and risk capital transactions, including investments and exit strategies; (4) divestments and spin-off of divisions or lines of business; (4) joint ventures and strategic alliances; (5) restructurings and reorganizations and distressed M&A, and (6) public stock offers, among others. While our work covers practically all industries and sectors, we have put particular emphasis on six of them: automotive & manufacturing, consumer goods, energy & natural resources, financial services, and pharmaceutical & life sciences and real estate. Within each of these industries we have organized complete and effective practice groups. The partners and lawyers that compose these groups are leaders in the respective industries and sectors. This multidisciplinary focus is centered on the needs of our clients and allows us to offer innovative and pragmatic legal advice, always tailored to the specific needs and goals of the companies we assist. The practice also has broad experience in the structuring and conducting of transactions in Latin America. For this, we coordinate with firms and lawyers with a proven track record in their countries and we thereby ensure the quality, consistency and continuity of the service in the operations. Prestigious publishers such as Chambers and Partners Global, Chambers and Partners Latin America, Legal 500, Latin Lawyer 250, IFLR 1000, Latin Lawyer National, and Lexology Index recognize and highlight the Corporate, Mergers & Acquisitions practice of Von Wobeser y Sierra.
