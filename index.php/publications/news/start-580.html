    Dr. Edmund Duckwitz - Dr. Edmund Duckwitz, Of Counsel of Von Wobeser y Sierra, S.C. has received the highest honor from the Mexican government “La Orden Mexicana del Águila Azteca en grado de banda”.
    December, 2015

    Dr. Edmund Duckwitz, Of Counsel at Von Wobeser y Sierra, S.C. has received the highest honor that a foreign official can receive on behalf of the Mexican government. Dr. Duckwitz, Former Ambassador of Germany to Mexico received the award from Sub secretary of Foreign Affairs, Carlos de Icaza.

    Dr. Duckwitz was decorated for his dedication and commitment in strengthening the commercial relationship between Germany and Mexico.  During his term he was a key figure in fostering new German businesses investing in Mexico.
