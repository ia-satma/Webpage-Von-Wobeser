    January 20th, 2014
     
    On January 1st 2014 Von Wobeser y Sierra S.C. ("VWyS") announced Diego Sierra as its newest Partner of the Firm, thus reaffirming its strategic, steady and organic growth.  Diego will head the Anticorruption & Compliance practice of VWyS advising international and national clients in their compliance efforts regarding Mexican anti-corruption rules as well as extra-territorial regulations such as the Foreign Corrupt Practices Act.  Diego Sierra will maintain the commercial litigation and international arbitration practice to which he has been committed throughout his career.  He is a Mexican attorney (also admitted to practice in New York) with international experience in New York (Skadden Arps (2011-2012)) who has been actively engaged in some of VWyS' most significant arbitrations and litigations of the past decade.

    Federal Act to Prevent and Identify Transactions Involving Illicit Funds.

    December 13th, 2013

    On Friday, December 13, 2013, the Tax Administration Service once again (as it did on November the 15th) notified, on its web page, the granting of a second extension to the deadline for the filing of Notices regarding the engaging in vulnerable activities, pursuant to Federal Act to Prevent and Identify Transactions Involving Illicit Funds. Therefore, the new deadline for the filing of said notices is January 17, 2014.

    To review this publication, please consult the Money Laundering Prevention Portal available in the web page https://sppld.sat.gob.mx/pld/index.html

    Energy Bill opens the Mexican energy market for foreign and national private entities
    December, 2013
    Energy Bill opens the Mexican energy market for foreign and national private entities.
     
    December 13th, 2013.
     
    Dear clients and friends,
     
    This December 10, 2013, with the vote of the majority of the political parties, the PRI, PAN, PVEM and PANAL, the plenary of the Senate approved the energy bill with 95 votes in favor and 28 against, which bill was also approved by the Deputies Chamber on December 11th at 11:05 p.m., in general, with 354 votes in favor and 134 against. Now it only remains for the majority of the local congresses to approve it, which would require the approval of 17 congresses of the 31 states and the Federal District of Mexico, and which is very likely to occur since the PRI holds the majority of the local congresses.

    Main modifications to tax provisions for the year 2014
    December, 2013
    Main Modifications to tax provisions for the year 2014.

    December 11th, 2013.

    Dear clients and friends,

    Dated today and December 9th, 2013, the modifications to tax laws passed by the Congress of the Union by the end of October were published in the Official Gazette of the Federation. Such publications make reference to the new Income Tax Law, revocation of the Corporate Flat Tax Rate Law and of the Tax on Cash Deposits Law, as well as the reform, repeal and addition of various provisions to the Value-added Tax Law, the Special Tax on Production and Services Law, the Law on Federal Fees, the Fiscal Code of the Federation and the Customs Law, respectively.
